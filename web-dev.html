Job Title: Web Developer

We are seeking a talented and experienced Web Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for web development and a strong understanding of front-end and back-end technologies. Responsibilities will include designing, coding, and modifying websites, from layout to function. Strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment are essential. If you thrive on challenges and are excited about creating innovative web solutions, we want to hear from you!

B.E degree in any field

65% above throughout their Academics

Proven experience as a Web Developer or similar role.

Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and at least one server-side scripting language.

Experience with responsive and mobile-first design principles.

Familiarity with version control systems such as Git.

Strong communication and collaboration skills.

Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks in a deadline-driven environment.

Knowledge of SEO principles and best practices is a plus.
